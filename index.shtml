Every undergraduate student involved in research has the potential to change the world around them. These students will be pioneers in both industry and academia. Many will strive to create entirely new fields and subfields of study. These students work with their community by collaborating with individuals in their fields to set up a venue and host a symposium where information and knowledge can be shared. The goal of STLAURS is to offer a unique opportunity where undergraduates can acquire these skills and understand how to build networks with the people around them.
