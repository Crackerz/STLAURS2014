Washington University in Saint Louis
Research is important to the academic community of Washington University. Through the collaboration of students and faculty, research is used to enhance the learning environment of the school and focus on important issues. There is also an emphasis on the variability of research being conducted with projects being conducted in most departments. Additionally, corporate involvement is also encouraged to advance the development of research.
Saint Louis University
At Saint Louis University, research is encouraged in a variety of disciplines and is considered an important aspect of learning for both undergraduate and graduate education. The research focus is basic, molecular, biomedical, clinical, epidemiological, educational as well as vaccine development. Two recent additions to the research culture are development of the Center for World Health and Medicine and new construction of the Doisy Research Center to house expanding research in biomedical and basic sciences.
Southern Illinois University Carbondale
Southern Illinois University Carbondale encourages the informative and discovery aspects of research. Through such aspects, the potential of research to contribute to a wider body of knowledge and the good of society is stressed. Present projects pertaining to various areas of study are centered in specific research centers and intend to expand inquiry. These projects pertain to areas such as ecology, biotechnology, and public policy.
Saint Louis College of Pharmacy
The research of Saint Louis College of Pharmacy is centered around the treatment of disease and the advancement of pharmaceutical education. Much of the research focuses on the development of drug therapies and patient-centered practices. Basic science research is also integrated into Saint Louis College of Pharmacy’s focus in order to better understand certain drugs’ characteristics and effects.
Southern Illinois University Edwardsville
Southern Illinois University Edwardsville has research programs that emphasize the creativity of the research process as well as the experience gained by such a process. This creativity is reflected in projects that entail topics such as cancer progression and literary evolution. Even more opportunities have opened up due to the National Institute of Health’s selection of Southern Illinois University Edwardsville as a center of excellence in pain education.
University of Missouri-Saint Louis
The focus of research for the University of Missouri-Saint Louis entails the preparation that the research experience will provide for further career opportunities. Further, the role of laboratory and field experience in the gain of knowledge is emphasized in the expectations of prospective researchers, especially those in the basic sciences. More recent endeavors include increasing opportunities in the biological sciences.
