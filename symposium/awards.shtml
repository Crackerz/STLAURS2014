Awards

The STLAURS Inter-School Board, in partnership with Southern Illinois University in Carbondale, is proud to offer cash awards and recognition to outstanding projects.
This years symposium will be broken into 8 catagories:
  • Humanities/Liberal Arts
  • Physical Science
  • Biological Science
  • Education
  • Art-Cinema/Mass Media
  • Engineering
  • Business
Each catagory will award $100 to its best of show. Second and third place posters will recieve certificates detailing their achievements.
